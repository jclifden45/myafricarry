✈ General
AfriCarryApp is a community-powered marketplace that connects Liberians traveling by air with senders who need to ship packages to Liberia affordably. Travelers post their travel dates and offer to carry packages for a fee. Senders browse available travelers and book a shipment — typically saving 60–70% compared to DHL or FedEx.
Traditional couriers charge $15–25 per kg to ship to Liberia. AfriCarryApp uses travelers who already have luggage space to carry packages for $8–15 per kg — significantly cheaper. The key differences:
  • Price: Up to 70% cheaper than DHL
  • Speed: Door-to-door in 5–14 days vs 10–21 days
  • Trust: Community ratings and reviews on every traveler
  • Protection: Escrow payments — your money held until delivery confirmed
AfriCarryApp currently serves Liberia — specifically Monrovia via Roberts International Airport (ROB) and Spriggs Payne Airport (MLW). We are actively planning expansion to Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, and Sierra Leone. Stay tuned!

It is simple:

💳 Payments & Escrow
Escrow protects both senders and travelers:
  • Sender pays → money is held securely by AfriCarryApp
  • Traveler carries the package and delivers it in Liberia
  • Sender confirms delivery → AfriCarryApp releases 90% to the traveler
  • If no confirmation is received within 7 days of the travel date, funds are released automatically
This means the traveler only gets paid after successful delivery, and the sender's money is protected throughout.
AfriCarryApp accepts all major payment methods through Stripe:
  • Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay
  • Debit cards
All payments are encrypted and processed securely. AfriCarryApp never stores your card details.
AfriCarryApp charges a 10% platform commission on every shipment. This is automatically deducted from the total payment before the traveler receives their payout.
Example: Sender pays $120 for a 10kg shipment at $12/kg.
AfriCarryApp keeps: $12 (10%)
Stripe processing fee: ~$3.78
Traveler receives: ~$104.22
The traveler receives their payout via Stripe within 2–7 business days after one of the following:
  • The sender clicks "Confirm Delivery" in the app
  • 7 days after the travel date passes with no dispute raised
Payouts go directly to the traveler's linked bank account.

📍 Delivery
Delivery time depends on when you find a traveler heading to Liberia. Once a traveler is booked:
  • Hand-off: Before the traveler's departure date
  • In-flight: Direct or connecting flights — typically 10–20 hours
  • Local delivery in Liberia: 1–3 days after arrival depending on recipient location
Total time: typically 5–14 days from booking to delivery — faster than most traditional couriers.
Once your recipient confirms they have received the package, go to the footer of the app and click "✅ Confirm Delivery". Enter your email and booking ID. Click "Yes, Delivered" — this releases the traveler's payment immediately. You have 48 hours after expected delivery to raise a dispute if needed.
If a traveler cancels their trip before departure, your payment is fully refunded automatically. You will receive an email notification and the refund will appear on your original payment method within 5–10 business days. You can then search for another available traveler on the platform.
