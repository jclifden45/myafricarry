Read carefully before booking a Full Luggage shipment. This disclaimer is legally binding.

AfriCarryApp is a peer-to-peer marketplace only. We do not inspect, handle, pack, seal, or take possession of any luggage or its contents at any point. We are not a courier, freight forwarder, or logistics provider.

⚠ 2. No Liability for Loss or Damage

AfriCarryApp is not responsible for any loss, theft, damage, or destruction of items in the luggage at any stage — including during packing, handover, transit, customs inspection, or delivery. The traveler and sender assume full risk.

🚫 3. No Liability for Illegal Items

AfriCarryApp is not responsible for any illegal, prohibited, or undeclared items found in the luggage. If customs authorities seize, confiscate, or fine either party due to the contents of the luggage, AfriCarryApp bears zero liability. The sender is solely responsible for ensuring all contents are legal under US, Liberian, and international law.

4. Traveler's Right to Refuse

The traveler has the absolute right to refuse to carry any luggage or item they are not comfortable with — at any time before departure — with no penalty. The traveler should inspect all contents before accepting the luggage.

5. Customs Declaration

The traveler must declare all items they carry on behalf of others at customs in both the US and Liberia. The sender must provide an accurate list of all contents and their declared values. Failure to declare items is a criminal offense.

6. Bag Fee Is Non-Refundable

The $200 airline checked bag fee included in the Full Luggage price is paid directly to the airline by the traveler. This fee is non-refundable once the bag is checked in, regardless of what happens to the contents.

7. Sender Confirms Full Knowledge of Contents

By booking a Full Luggage shipment, the sender confirms they personally packed the luggage, are fully aware of all contents, and confirm that nothing prohibited, illegal, or undeclared has been included.

8. Indemnification

The sender and traveler agree to indemnify and hold harmless AfriCarryApp, its founders, employees, and affiliates from any claims, fines, penalties, legal action, or losses arising from the Full Luggage shipment.
